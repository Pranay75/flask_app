About Us

This Cybersecurity Portal is maintained by the Cybersecurity Lab associated with Prof. N.S. Narayanaswamy and Prof Chester.

How It Works

We regularly pose questions that are of interest to the cybersecurity community. Participants are encouraged to solve these questions and submit their solutions via email to rise.research.labs@gmail.com.

Submission Guidelines

  • Each solution must include a comprehensive explanation of the approach and methodology used.
  • A video demonstrating the Proof of Concept (PoC) must accompany the written explanation.

Evaluation

Submitted solutions will be evaluated by our team. Students whose solutions seem promising will be invited to the lab to present their work. Based on the impact and quality of the solutions, marks will be awarded.

Ranking and Certificate

The Honor Board will be updated after our team evaluates the solutions. At the end of the year, a certificate mentioning your ranking for the current year will be issued. This can be used when applying for internships or placements.
